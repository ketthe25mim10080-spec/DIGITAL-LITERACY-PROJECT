# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India | CSE0001 – Digital Literacy | Vivek Varma | 25MIM10080

---

## General Online Safety

1. **Use strong, unique passwords** – Never reuse the same password across multiple platforms. Use a passphrase or a password manager.
2. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** – Activate 2FA on all important accounts including email, GitHub, LinkedIn, and banking apps.
3. **Keep your devices and apps updated** – Security patches in updates protect against known vulnerabilities.
4. **Never click on links in unsolicited messages** – Whether received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email — always verify the source before clicking.
5. **Log out of shared devices** – Always log out of your accounts when using college computers or shared devices.
6. **Use only trusted, secure Wi-Fi networks** – Avoid entering passwords or banking details over public or unverified Wi-Fi.

---

## Financial / UPI Safety (Mandatory)

7. **Understand the difference between "Send" and "Receive"** – In UPI, you should NEVER need to scan a QR code or enter your PIN to *receive* money. If someone asks you to do so, it is a scam.
8. **Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or CVV with anyone** – No bank, company, or government authority will ever ask for these over a call or message.

---

## Social & Platform Safety

9. **Verify profiles before connecting** – Do not accept connection requests or reply to messages from unverified or unknown accounts.
10. **Report cyberbullying and harassment immediately** – Use in-app reporting tools and, if serious, report to the cybercrime portal.

---

## Where to Report Cybercrime in India

| Resource | Details |
|---|---|
| **National Cyber Crime Portal** | cybercrime.gov.in – Report all forms of cybercrime online |
| **Cyber Crime Helpline** | **1930** – Available 24x7 for financial fraud and cybercrime |
| **Local Police** | File an FIR at your nearest cybercrime cell if losses are significant |

---

*Your safety is in your hands. Stay alert, stay informed.*
